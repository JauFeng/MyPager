Frequently Asked Questions


What can the doctors handle?

  • Doctors provide three types of visits: urgent care visits, physicals, and flu shots.
  • Doctors are able to treat a wide range of urgent care needs. For the full list of medical conditions treated by our doctors, please click here.
  • Doctors treat both adults and children.
  • PLEASE NOTE THAT DOCTORS ON PAGER SHOULD NOT BE SOLICITED FOR EMERGENCY CARE. IF YOU ARE HAVING EMERGENCY, PLEASE DIAL 911.
  • DOCTORS CANNOT PRESCRIBE NARCOTICS OR SCHEDULED DRUGS.

How do you find the doctors?

  • Pager recruits and trains doctors who are board-certified and NY licensed. They have been independently verified and credentialed to ensure the best care for our community.
  • Use the Pager app to find a nearby doctor who is available for a visit. Before selecting a doctor, you’ll be able to see the following information: their bio, medical credentials, photo, and price.
  • See a list of the doctors here.

When and where can I request a Pager visit?

Care is available every day in the New York City area from 8 am to 10 pm. Not in NYC? Register now and be the first to know when Pager comes to you.

What do I provide to request a visit?

To register, you will need to provide the following:
  • Full Name
  • Date of birth
  • Email
  • Phone Number
  • Address
  • Valid Credit Card Information
Your account information is completely secure and private. For more information, please check out our Privac Policy.

Can I request a visit for my child?

  • If you are requesting a visit for a child/minor, please swipe left at the top of the app screen to add your child’s information in. After, request a visit and a doctor will be able to take care of your child’s needs.
  • You can continuing swiping to add any additional children.

Do doctors make referrals?

  • Your care may require some additional services or treatment outside the customary house call. Your doctor will notify you of any recommended additional services at the time of your visit. The decision is yours on whether to accept the additional services.
  • Referrals for additional care from other health care providers or institutions are at the discretion of your doctor.

Pricing

Pricing is based on the type of visit. The total price of your visit includes all fees (doctor, transportation, etc). If additional services are necessary, you will be notified. Upon completion of your visit, you will receive an email invoice for charges applied to your credit card.
  • First-time Urgent Care Visit: $50
  • Regular Urgent Care Visit: $200
  • Phone Consultation: $25
  • Physicals: $100
  • Prescription Delivery: $0

Insurance

Pager is an out-of-network provider. Please check our Pricing page for additional information. We’re able to help you submit for reimbursement and quickly working toward becoming an in-network provider.

Cancellation Policy

If you cancel more than five minutes after your doctor has confirmed the visit, you will be charged a $10 cancellation fee.
