What is a Health Check?

The Pager Health Check is a short screening performed by a Registered Nurse to give you an assessment of your overall health and wellness. The Health Check will identify your risk for heart disease, stroke, and diabetes – three of the most common health problems in the United States.

Empower yourself and your family by knowing your numbers and minimizing your risks. Pager makes it convenient by coming to you, so you can avoid the hassle of the doctor’s office and busy waiting room.

What Does A Health Check Include?

Consultation

Review of Family and Medical History to Assess Your Risk


Body Measurements

Height, Weight and
Body Mass Index (BMI)


Immediate Labwork

Instant results for Cholesterol, Triglycerides, Glucose and Blood Pressure

Schedule and Prepare for Your Health Check

8 Hour Fast

Lab readings are most accurate after eight hours of fasting. Appointments in the morning, pre-breakfast, are ideal. If you cannot fast, you may still get your Health Check, but it might not be the most accurate.
