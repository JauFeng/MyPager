How Does it Work?

The Pager app takes care of everything so you can focus on getting better.

1.

Request a visit at the tap of a button. The doctor will call you to assess your need.


2.

Doctor visits, diagnoses, treats and prescribes medication as needed. Prescription delivery available upon request.


3.

The doctor will follow-up with you the next day via call or message.
